WELCOME

Setup

There is a 6-row, 7-column board. You have the option to pick between a red piece or a yellow piece.

Objective

Be the first to place your piece consecutively 4 times. You can do it horizontally, vertically, or even diagonally. Pick your piece, make your move! Let the best connect all!
